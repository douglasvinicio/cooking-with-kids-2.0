Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I print a recipe?

A: Not yet. But we're implementing this function and it will be available soon.

Q: Can other relatives be a part of this?

A: Why not. Having an aunt/uncle or grandparent in the bonding works too.

Q: What type of flour should I use for gluten-free recipes?

A: Most recipes will specify which flour to use: medium-grain rice flour, short "sweet" rice flour, regular long-grain, or brown rice. Otherwise, you can always just roll with gluten-free all-purpose flour.

Q: What is the age range for kids?

A: Ages 6 and up. Parental guidance is required.

Q: Where do you get your recipes?

A: The website uses pre-existing recipes from all over the web (aimed at younger audiences).
